# HealthyMe Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 19, 2025**

HealthyMe ("the App") is developed by Michael Miles ("Developer", "we", "us"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Summary

**Your health data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or health information to external servers.**

## Data We Access

HealthyMe requests read-only access to the following Apple Health data to provide personalized wellness insights:

### Heart Metrics
- Resting heart rate
- Walking heart rate average
- Heart rate variability (HRV)

### Sleep Data
- Sleep duration
- Sleep stages (REM, Core, Deep)
- Sleep efficiency
- Awakenings count

### Activity & Performance
- Step count
- Walking/running distance
- Active calories burned
- Exercise minutes
- VO2 max
- Walking speed
- Six-minute walk test distance

### Body Measurements
- Weight
- Body Mass Index (BMI)
- Respiratory rate
- Blood oxygen saturation
- Overnight wrist temperature

### Mindfulness
- Mindfulness session duration and count

## How We Use Your Data

All health data accessed by HealthyMe is:

1. **Processed entirely on your device** using Apple's on-device Foundation Models (Apple Intelligence)
2. **Used solely to generate personalized wellness insights** shown within the App
3. **Never transmitted to external servers** â€” we have no backend servers
4. **Never shared with third parties**
5. **Never used for advertising or marketing**
6. **Never sold**

## Data Storage

- Health data is cached locally on your device to improve performance
- Cached data is stored using Apple's secure on-device storage
- Uninstalling the App removes all cached data from your device
- We do not write any data back to Apple Health

## Third-Party Services

HealthyMe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App operates entirely offline after installation.

## Children's Privacy

HealthyMe does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App is intended for general audiences.

## Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device:
- You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > HealthyMe
- You can delete all App data by uninstalling HealthyMe
