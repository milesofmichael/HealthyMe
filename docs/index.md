# HealthyMe

HealthyMe is an iOS app that integrates Apple Health data with on-device AI to provide personalized wellness insights.

## Links

- [Privacy Policy](privacy)
- [Report an Issue](https://github.com/milesofmichael/HealthyMe/issues)
- [View Source Code](https://github.com/milesofmichael/HealthyMe)

## About

HealthyMe reads your health data (heart rate, sleep, activity, body measurements, mindfulness) and uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models to show you trends and insights. All processing happens privately on your device.
